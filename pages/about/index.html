About Me
[Education]
I graduated from National Taiwan University of Arts, majored in Crafts & Design.
In the department I studied the artistry and practical applications of design and learned how to make objects with different materials, such as ceramic, metal, fabric, stained glass and leather.

I have a passion for materializing my own design, especially for leather crafting. Besides the course in university, I also had a part-time job in a leather studio at the same time, which brought me the experience of selling, introducing and making custom leather products.

During the semesters, to develop and present the concept of creative works, we did a lot of thinking exercise, and that extends to sort out the attitude toward life. For example, the initial motivation for choosing this department is that I tend to acquire how to make practical items by hand, but gained a deeper understanding afterward that we don't need so many things in our lives.


[After Graduation]
In addition to the stereoscopic creation, I took some extra courses in graphic design and learned preliminary website design method as well. When I just graduated, I know I didn't want to make or print out any entity object at that moment, I decided to become a visual designer for website design. Therefore, I learned CSS3, HTML5 and a bit of JavaScript by myself.


[The First Job - Website Design]
In the same year of graduation, I started to work at a multimedia marketing company in Taichung, where I accumulated the experiences of website layout for two years, I have completed more than 100 projects of modular web design.


[Second Job - Graphic Design]
I moved to Taipei in 2018, working for a biotechnology company as a visual designer, my job is to design the identity of their health supplements or ODM products, banners for E-commerce channels and modify the briefs for PM. Any visual-related project is my scope of work.
I did share my resources-wasting perspective with the manager in the job interview, she told me their business inevitably has to produce some physical products, but the company already put their main operating strategy to e-commerce channels, that means except the simple-structured packaging, they no longer print out flyers or advertising board, so during my tenure the graphics are all released in electronic format, which is an acceptable situation for me.


[A New Journey]
After worked for another year, I applied the working-holiday visa to Belgium in 2020, look forward to expanding my vision in this journey.
My education background and all the industries of my employments seem not so related, but the common point is, they're all about visual design, that's my enthusiasm.
