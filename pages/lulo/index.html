Bring Me Back
LULO
[2020]
I designed the visual identity for the graduation exhibition of our department.
The exhibition called LULO. In Chinese, the two characters mean "dewdrops just showing up".
All the exhibits were made by hand, so I used a lot of handwriting elements on it.
The strokes sembled with western alphabets. They are different from each other, but assembled into a new meaning, just like us.
