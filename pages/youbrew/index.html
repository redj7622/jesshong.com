Bring Me Back
Youbrew Tea
[2020]
This was my first case in Belgium, the client was a startup tea company from England.
Their tea series needed 7 different stickers, in organic and comfortable style.

I distinguish the 7 products by different tones of color, keep the same simple structure as their logo, use paper-cutting style elements to build the layers and add some natural feeling by handcrafted edge in the graphics.
The patterns are based on tea leaves, lavender, mint, chrysanthemum and oregano.
