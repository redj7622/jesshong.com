Yen's Seafood
[2017]
This logo was designed for my cusine’s seafood delivery service.
Her parents run a fresh seafood trading business in Penghu, and she sells frozen seafood in Kaohsiung.

I designed a minimalist mark with lines and eye-catching colors, which is easy to associate with fish, ocean and the brand name.
This logo is also suitable as stickers or printed on t-shirt and flag.
