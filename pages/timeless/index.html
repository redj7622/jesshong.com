Bring Me Back
Timeless
[2019]
I desgned this logo and the layout of packagings for a startup beauty care products company in Taiwan.
Target audience of the product are women around 30, my client hopes I could combine the clock element with the Chinese characters of the brand name, and keep everything simple, for leaving some space for the pearl paper they want to use on the packaging.

The final version is the one made with Tsuku rounded gothic font and clock pointer.
The pointer stays the same positions of clock surface and replaces some stroke of the characters.
In another logo formats, the pointer joins the english characters as well.
